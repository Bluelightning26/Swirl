Who?

All Hack Clubbers! Anyone, anywhere, who is 18 under, or is in High School in the USA!
Getting your Boba Drops submission approved is required to submit!



What?

Swirl is the next step after the completion of Boba Drops! You are expected to make a website that is packed with cool features and has something fun and unique!
It’s your time to shine and push yourself farther than ever before!
See the requirements page for more information.



How?

Swirl will be more independent than Boba Drops, as it is all about you making your own unique website!
Cool features can be found all over the internet, and it is encouraged for you to do research on specific attributes you may want to add, just remember to not copy-paste anything, and don't claim other's code as your own!
There is a guide dedicated to Swirl and it is recommended that you check it out!
